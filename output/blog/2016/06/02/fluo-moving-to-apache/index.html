Fluo is moving to Apache

02 Jun 2016

Twitter

Fluo was recently proposed as an Apache Podling. The vote passed and now we are in the process of moving to Apache. See INFRA-11900 for more details.

Currently the only thing setup in Apache land is the mailing list. If you would like to subscribe to the new list, send an email to dev-subscribe@fluo.incubator.apache.org. Archives of the list are also available.
